Pizza

Pizza is pretty baller

This pizza is simple and delicious for the whole family

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of flout
  • t teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. mix first 4 ingredients in a large bowl
  2. mix water and oil and add to mixture
  3. knead out dough and then let rise for 20 min
  4. punch down and shape into flat circle
  5. add tomato sauce
  6. add cheese
  7. bake at 400 degrees for 20 min
