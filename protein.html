Protein bars! :)

These home made protein bars will get you swole!

These protein bars are a great snack to makes for any gym goer or anyone who is wanting to change their eating habits to be a bit healthier

Ingredients

  • 10 dates
  • 4 scoops of protein powder
  • 1 cup of salted nuts
  • 2 tbsp of chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 1/4 cup of peanut butter
  • 2/3 cup of oat milk

Steps

  1. add rolled oates to food processor and make into fine powder
  2. Add nuts and protein powder and combine
  3. add chia seeds, peanut butter, dates, and syrup
  4. process it all together
  5. add milk
  6. line cake pan with parchment paper and form bars on it
  7. cut the bars into shape
  8. If you want chocolate coating then melt some chocolate chips and add thin chocolate shell
