Simple chicken like grandma used to make

This is just a simple chicken recipe that is easy to prepare and only takes a few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Creole seasoning
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Coat chicken with olive oil and sprinkle both sides with salt and seasoning
  2. Bake in a broiler pan for about 10 minutes and then flip and bake for 15 more minutes
  3. pour chicken broth into pan and deglaze. Drizzle pan sauce over chicken before serving
